FAQ
Common questions.
How do I give you access to my server?
You just add me as an admin temporarily. I'll set everything up and you can remove my access once you're happy with the result. I never touch anything I'm not supposed to.
How do I pay?
Payment is handled through Discord. Message me at @tealgaveup and I'll send you the payment details. I accept PayPal and most major methods.
How long does setup take?
Most servers are fully set up within 24 hours of ordering. Basic servers are often done within just a few hours.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
I'll revise it until you're satisfied. Just message me and tell me what you'd like changed — I want you to love the final result.
Can I request something custom not in the packages?
Absolutely. Message me on Discord and describe what you need — custom bots, unique role structures, special layouts — and I'll give you a quote.
